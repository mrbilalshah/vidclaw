Features

Everything you need to manage your AI agent from one self-hosted dashboard. No cloud, no accounts, no tracking.

Self-Hosted AI Dashboard

VidClaw is a self-hosted AI dashboard that runs entirely on your own server. Unlike cloud-based alternatives, your data never leaves your machine. The dashboard binds to localhost and is accessed through an SSH tunnel ‚Äî giving you a secure, private command center for your AI agent.

Built with React and Node.js, VidClaw is lightweight, fast, and designed for developers who prefer to own their infrastructure. No subscriptions, no vendor lock-in, no third-party dependencies.

OpenClaw Agent Management

VidClaw is purpose-built for OpenClaw agent management. It reads directly from your OpenClaw workspace, giving you a visual interface over everything your agent does.

AI Cost Tracking

AI cost tracking is built into VidClaw's core. Monitor real-time token usage and cost estimates across all your AI models. Visual progress bars match Anthropic's rate limit windows so you always know where you stand.

  • Per-model breakdown ‚Äî see costs for Claude, GPT, and other models separately
  • Daily and monthly summaries ‚Äî track spending trends over time
  • Rate limit awareness ‚Äî progress bars show how close you are to limits
  • Model switching ‚Äî change models directly from the navbar

Activity Calendar & History

A monthly calendar view shows what your agent has been working on each day. Data is parsed from memory files and task completion history, giving you a bird's-eye view of your agent's productivity.

Privacy & Security First

VidClaw is 100% self-hosted with zero cloud dependencies. The dashboard only binds to localhost ‚Äî you access it through an SSH tunnel. No accounts, no analytics (except optional Plausible), no data collection. Your agent's data stays on your server.
